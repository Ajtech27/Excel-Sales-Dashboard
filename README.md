## ğŸ” Key Insights

### Revenue by Occasion
- **Anniversary** and **Raksha Bandhan** generate the highest revenue.
- **Diwali** contributes less revenue compared to other festive occasions.

### Revenue by Category
- **Colors** and **Soft Toys** are the top revenue-generating categories.
- **Plants** and **Mugs** show lower sales performance.

### Top Products by Revenue
Top 5 products contributing the most revenue:
1. Magnum Set  
2. Quia Gift  
3. Dolores Gift  
4. Harum Pack  
5. Dessert Box  

### Monthly Revenue Trend
- Revenue peaks in **February** and **August**, indicating seasonal demand.
- Revenue remains relatively stable but lower during mid-year months.

### Orders by City
- Cities such as **Imphal, Kavali, and Dibrugarh** record the highest number of orders.
- Some cities show low order volume, presenting growth opportunities.

### Revenue by Hour (Order Time)
- **Evening hours** generate higher revenue.
- Early-day orders show comparatively lower sales activity.
